# Retail Sales Analysis (Python & Power BI)

##  Business Problem

Retail businesses must understand sales performance, product demand, and customer purchasing patterns to maximise revenue and optimise inventory management.

This project analyses retail transaction data to identify key sales trends, high-performing products, and business growth opportunities.

---

##  Objectives

- Analyse overall sales performance
- Identify top-selling products and categories
- Evaluate seasonal and monthly sales trends
- Understand customer purchasing behaviour
- Provide data-driven business recommendations

---

##  Tools Used

- **Python:** Pandas, NumPy
- **Data Visualisation:** Matplotlib, Seaborn
- **Power BI:** Interactive KPI dashboard)

---

##  Dataset Overview

The dataset includes:
- Transaction ID
- Product category
- Sales amount
- Quantity sold
- Customer information
- Order date
- Region / Store location (if available)

---

##  Data Cleaning & Preparation

- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing values
- Converted date columns to datetime format
- Created calculated features (monthly revenue, category totals, etc.)

---

##  Key Analyses

- Total revenue and sales growth over time
- Monthly and seasonal sales trends
- Top-performing products
- Category-level contribution analysis
- Sales distribution across regions/stores

---

##  Key Insights

- Sales show strong seasonal variation with peak periods during specific months.
- A small number of products contribute significantly to overall revenue.
- Certain categories consistently outperform others.
- Regional differences indicate potential market expansion opportunities.
